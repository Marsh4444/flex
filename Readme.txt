# ⚙️ Mass ↔ Weight Converter

A simple Python CLI tool that converts **mass (kg)** to **weight (N)** and vice versa.

---

### 🧩 Features

* Converts between kilograms and newtons.
* Handles invalid inputs gracefully.
* Loops until you decide to quit.
* Lightweight, beginner-friendly, and perfect for practice.

---

### 🚀 How It Works

1. Run the script.
2. Choose what to convert —

   * `m` → convert *weight to mass*
   * `w` → convert *mass to weight*
3. Enter your number and get the result instantly.

---

### 🧠 Formula Used

* **Weight (N)** = Mass (kg) × 10
* **Mass (kg)** = Weight (N) ÷ 10

---

### 💡 Example

```
Convert to Mass or Weight? (m/w): w
Enter the Mass of the object (kg): 15
✅ The Weight is 150.0 N
```
